Overview

Use the side bar to navigate between different family tree filters ( "Students", "Students & Instructors", "Instructional Staff", "Internship Network", and "Capstone Technologies").

Family Tree Features

  • Node Summaries: Double-click on tree nodes to zoom in and read information on a member, company, cohort, capstone technology.
  • Node Lables:You may hover over nodes to display thier titles OR use the label filters in the side bar.
  • Draggable Nodes: Click and hold down on a node to move its position.
  • Draggable Canvas:Click on empty canvas space (outside of node/tree) and drag the canvas to re-center your view.
  • Night Mode: Switch allows you to toggle between light or dark displays.
  • Zoom & Panning: Use scroll or touchpad to zoom and resize your view of the visuals.

Mission

Welcome and thank you for using the Ada Family Tree! This web application aims to accomplish two goals:

  • Visually map the growth of the Ada social network,
  • Help others in the Ada family connect and learn more about members of our Ada community

If you have suggestions or would like to send feedback for improvement, please send all questions and inquiries to: jedrozo@gmail.com.

About

Creation of the Ada Family Tree was motivated primarily by Adaâ€™s legacy wall of photos and, by a general collective desire to improve means of connecting and learning from and more about our Adie predecessors. For many of us, we've learned about former Adies from the "wall" of photos, meet-ups, occasional anecdotes, linkedIn, and of course google, but prior to the webapp, there wasn't a readily available resource that documented all Adies and former/current ADA staff who are or were a part of the ADA family. The Ada Family Tree was built and hopes to facilitate the documentation and bridging of connection gaps in Ada's ever-growing social network.

Privacy

To respect privacy concerns, currently only Ada staff and approved users (e.g. Ada Admin Staff and developers of this tool) of the Ada community may view or edit the family tree. Until further notice, Ada Staff may share the family tree with their class or students on a need-to-know-basis or when valid reason is warranted.
